STEP 1 - Brand questionnaire

We will send you a list of questions to clarify your brand concept. The branding questionnaire consists of: general questions, in depth interview, the market, the target, the identity.

STEP 2 - Mood-boards proposals

Creation of initial brand concepts and mood boards. We will define the tone of the brand by visually illustrating the general look and feel for 3 different visual concepts. (1 revision)

STEP 3 - Logo design variations

Based on the mood-board selected we will present 4 logo options, each logo will be completely different and will include various font/type/layout and styles. (2 revisions)

STEP 4 - Brand style-board - This will be your visual bible!.

Once the logo has been finalized we will build a brand style-board which will includethe logo, color palette, a secondary logo option, a sub-mark or brand element, 2 font recommendations, and 2 graphic elements. (1 revision)
