STEP 1

Brand questionnaire

We will send you a list of questions to clarify your brand concept. The branding questionnaire consists of: general questions, in depth interview, the market, the target, the identity.

STEP 2

Mood-boards proposals

Creation of initial brand concepts and mood boards. We will define the tone of the brand by visually illustrating the general look and feel for 3 different visual concepts.

One revision

STEP 3

Logo design variations

Based on the mood-board selected we will present 4 logo options, each logo will be completely different and will include various font/type/layout and styles.

Two revisions

STEP 4

Brand style-board - This will be your visual bible!.

Once the logo has been finalized we will build a brand style-board which will includethe logo, color palette, a secondary logo option, a sub-mark or brand element, 2 font recommendations, and 2 graphic elements.

One revision

STEP 5

FINAL FILES DELIVERY

You will receive the logo, secondary logo, and submark/elements in .jpg, .png, .png transparent background, .eps, and a watermark. You will receive the color names in RGB, CMYK, and HEX. You will also receive the patterns in 12”x12” at 300dpi JPEG.
